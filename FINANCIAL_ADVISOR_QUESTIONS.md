## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: "How do I save for a house down payment?" vs "Tell me about saving"
2. **Include Context**: "I make $5k/month and spend $4k, should I invest or save?"
3. **Ask Follow-ups**: Get more details on any topic
4. **Use Your Numbers**: The AI analyzes your actual financial data
5. **Explore Options**: Ask about pros and cons of different strategies

The AI Financial Advisor provides personalized advice based on your actual financial data including income, expenses, balance, and savings rate!
